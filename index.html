WireFrame

Some wireframe basic styles. IMO the proportions look best with the editor docked on the side on a wide screen monitor. Used Normalize.css. Added some content like nav, nav-position, and the padding on Main just to fill it out a little. You get the idea, right?

BTW when you wireframe with CSS you must put sizes on block elements since there's a lack of content to make the blocks bigger.
